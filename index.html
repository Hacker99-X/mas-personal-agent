SMS / Text Messaging Privacy Policy

Mas Personal Agent · Last updated: June 21, 2026

This policy describes how Mas Personal Agent ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our SMS text messaging program. By opting in, you agree to the terms below.

1. The information we collect

When you use our text messaging service, we collect your mobile phone number and the contents of the messages you send to and receive from our service. We use this information solely to operate the messaging service you have opted into.

We do not share your mobile information. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third parties. Information may only be disclosed to subcontractors strictly as needed to support the service itself (for example, the messaging platform that delivers texts), and never for their own marketing.

2. How we use your information

We use your mobile number and messages only to provide and operate the text messaging service you requested, to respond to your messages, and to send service-related information consistent with the program you opted into.

3. Message frequency

Message frequency varies. The number of messages you receive depends on your interactions with the assistant, including reminders, replies, and summaries you request.

4. Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply. These charges are set by your mobile carrier and are your responsibility. Contact your carrier for details about your plan.

5. Opting out and getting help

You can cancel the SMS service at any time by replying STOP. After you send STOP, we will send a confirmation message and then stop sending messages. To rejoin, sign up again as you did originally.

For help, reply HELP or contact us at masonhacker@gmail.com.

6. How you opted in

Mas Personal Agent is a personal assistant used by its account owner. You provided consent to receive text messages from the service during account setup and by initiating contact with the assistant. The service is operated for the registered owner only. Consent to receive messages is not a condition of any purchase.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

8. Contact us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at masonhacker@gmail.com.
